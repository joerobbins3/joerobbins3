Poets

Frederico Garcia Lorca

"So, then, the duende is a force not a labour, a struggle not a thought.
heard an old maestro of the guitar say: ‘The duende is not in the throat:
duende surges up, inside, from the soles of the feet.’ Meaning, it’s not a
question of skill, but of a style that’s truly alive: meaning, it’s in the
veins: meaning, it’s of the most ancient culture of immediate creation."
